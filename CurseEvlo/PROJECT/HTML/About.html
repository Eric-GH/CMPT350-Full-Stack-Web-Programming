Welcome to Learn The Courses!

This website only for Students && Teachers in University of Saskatchewan right now.

In this website, you can easy find all the specific course describes from our lovely users.

To start use the website, you can just click the "search" icon, then enter the course code(which you want to search) to find the course.

If you like to leave your own common for the course, the best way is click the menu tag, select the "Sign Up" tag, and create your own account in our website, or if you already have one, you can just select "Log In" tag to log in your account. After that, everytime you search the course, you can find out a leave common box at the end of the old commons, that's the place you can leave your own common.


Again, Thank you to use the Learn The Courses website. Hoping you have a good day!
